How to Breakdown the Problem?

Step 2: Break Down the Problem

▶ Break down the problem in Step 1 and sort it into smaller and more concrete problems in order to find a root cause effectively and efficiently.


▶ One need to be careful because there is a tendency to proceed to the next without breaking down the problem sufficiently.

Step 2: Details of the Process and Key Points

01
Break Down the Problem (GAP)

● A big problem into smaller problems from various angle
(connected 1.12, 1.2, 1.3…)
(optional 1, 2, 3.1, 3.2)

● Sort out all doubtful issues

● Classify problems into categories (Division Point) of “What-Where-When-Who”

02
Prioritize the broken down problem

03
Specify the Point of Occurrence by checking the process

● Examine (draw) the process to find in which process the problem has occurred (The Point of Occurrence) through Genchi Genbutsu and interviews.
